FAQ

Find answers to common questions about ULLU Zero products and their functionality.

How does ULLU Zero work?

ULLU Zero uses a combination of a physical device that plugs into a car's USB port and a smartphone app. It opens gates and garages based on user location and access status, allowing users to enter without the need for passwords or buttons. The device communicates with the cloud to retrieve access codes and transmits them to the gate or garage.

What are the features?

ULLU Zero offers features such as auto open and close for gates and garages, secure access sharing, comprehensive control over access points, and the ability to facilitate access for autonomous vehicles.

How can I share access with others?

ULLU Zero allows users to securely share access to their property with family members, guests, and service providers. This ensures controlled and convenient access without compromising security.

Can autonomous vehicles use ULLU Zero?

Yes, ULLU Zero enables autonomous vehicles to enter gated neighborhoods without manual intervention. This helps overcome the current limitation of autonomous vehicles not being able to access gated areas.
